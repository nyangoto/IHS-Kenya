How Prequalification Works

A transparent, three-stage process — fully documented, free from discretion. Qualified contractors know exactly where they stand at every step.

1. Eligibility Gate

Instant automated checks against NCA and KRA registries. No manual back-and-forth — you'll know in seconds if you qualify to proceed.

2. Compliance Review

Our team validates your uploaded documents against official registries. Expect a decision within 5 working days — with clear feedback if anything needs addressing.

3. Technical Approval

A Quantity Surveyor reviews your project history and financial capacity. Successful applicants receive their Prequalification Certificate by email.

IHS Kenya develops institutional housing projects across the country. Our digital prequalification portal connects verified contractors directly to multi-million shilling construction opportunities — fairly and transparently.

Core Eligibility Requirements

Please ensure you have digital copies of the following mandatory documents before starting your application.

Tax Compliance

Valid KRA Tax Compliance Certificate (TCC) updated for the current period.

Legal Status

Certificate of Incorporation and latest CR12 (not older than 6 months).

Ethical Standards

Signed Anti-Corruption Declaration and Code of Conduct compliance.

Technical Depth

Proof of equipment, relevant licenses (NCA, etc.), and key personnel CVs.

Financial Health

Audited financial statements for the last 2 years from certified practitioners.

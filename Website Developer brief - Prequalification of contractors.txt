Website Developer brief – Online contractor Prequalification tab on IHS-Kenya website
1.0 Objective
To develop a publicly accessible prequalification enlistment portal on the company website that:
* Transparently publishes enlistment criteria,
* Allows contractors to apply for enlistment online,
* Restricts enlistment to firms legally able to operate in Kenya, and
* Supports internal procurement governance and audit requirements.
The system is intended for enlistment for prequalification only and does not guarantee prequalification invitation, tender invitations or contract awards.
2.0 Criteria for enlisting
      2.1 Legal & Statutory compliance
* Copy of Business Registration or Certificate of Incorporation
* Copy of CR12 for Limited Companies
* Copy of KRA PIN Certificate
* Copy of valid Tax Compliance Certificate
* Copy of Registration with National Construction Authority (NCA) for Building works Contractor
* Copy of Valid Contractor Annual Practicing License from National Construction Authority (NCA) Building Works Contractor
      2.2 Relevant Kenyan experience
* Contractors must demonstrate proven experience delivering building projects within Kenya. This shall include:
* A minimum of three (3) completed projects undertaken in Kenya within the last five (5) years
* Experience on projects of comparable scale, complexity, and typology (e.g. residential, affordable housing, mixed-use)
* Clear indication of the contractor’s role as main contractor
Projects executed outside Kenya may be provided for reference but will not substitute for Kenyan experience.
      
      2.3 Enlistment Conditions
* Enlistment is subject to internal evaluation and approval.
* The register of prequalified contractors is reviewed periodically.
* Enlistment does not guarantee invitation for prequalification, to tender or award of any contract.
* IHS reserves the right to request additional information or reject any application without obligation to provide reasons

Submission Tips

Fiscal Year Alignment

NCA annual practicing licenses expire on 30th June each year. Ensure your license covers the current financial year.

Verification

License serial numbers are verified against NCA's public register. Ensure the number is entered correctly.

Legibility

Ensure the QR code on your license is clearly visible in the PDF scan for automated verification.

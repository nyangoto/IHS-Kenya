Data Processing & Privacy

In accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, IHS Kenya Limited collects and processes personal and corporate data solely for the purpose of contractor and supplier prequalification. Your information will be stored securely, used only for verification and compliance assessment, and will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent or as required by law.

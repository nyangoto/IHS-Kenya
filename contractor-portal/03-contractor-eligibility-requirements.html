Eligibility Requirements

Review the following requirements for Civil Works Contractor before proceeding.

Valid KRA Tax Compliance Certificate

Must be current and valid. Certificate number verified against KRA iTax records.

Certificate of Incorporation

Official registration with the Registrar of Companies.

CR12 Document

Latest CR12 not older than 6 months, listing all directors and shareholders.

NCA Registration Certificate

Valid National Construction Authority registration in the relevant category.

NCA Annual Practicing License

Current annual practicing license for the relevant financial year.

KRA PIN Certificate

Valid KRA Personal Identification Number certificate for the registered entity.

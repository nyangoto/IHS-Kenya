Guidance Note

What is KRA PIN?

An 11-digit alphanumeric identifier issued by the Kenya Revenue Authority to every registered taxpayer. It is your unique tax identification number used for all tax-related transactions.

Why do we need this?

Verification of legal standing and tax registration status is a mandatory compliance requirement for all prequalification applications.
